Role 1 of 8 — Priority #1
Director of Product
Product — Cross-workstream delivery lead
CriticalBy W2
Role Summary

Owns cross-workstream coordination, delivery management, and stakeholder communications across all 5 parallel workstreams. Reports to Head of Stealth Labs. This is the #1 hire — without this role, Anton is a single point of failure across delivery, hiring, and stakeholder management.

Responsibilities
  • Own day-to-day delivery management across all 5 workstreams (UC1, UC2, UC3, Rebrand, AWS Migration)
  • Run sprint ceremonies, stand-ups, and cross-team dependency resolution
  • Manage hiring pipeline alongside Head of Stealth Labs — screen candidates, coordinate interviews, close offers
  • Own backlog prioritisation and trade-off decisions when workstreams compete for shared resources
  • Coordinate with Diligent stakeholders — workshop prep, API spec follow-ups, integration alignment
  • Track and escalate risks from the risk register; own mitigation execution
  • Prepare weekly status reports and board-level summaries for Diligent leadership
Requirements
  • 8+ years in product management, with 3+ in a Director or Senior PM role
  • Experience managing multiple parallel workstreams in a startup or scale-up environment
  • Strong technical literacy — can engage with engineering on architecture, API design, and infrastructure decisions
  • GRC, audit, or compliance domain experience is a strong plus
  • Proven ability to operate autonomously and make trade-off decisions under ambiguity
  • Experience with AI/ML product delivery is highly desirable
  • London-based or willing to relocate; on-site at Diligent London office
